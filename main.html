monthly news letter

Nike is introducing a virtual sneaker store and trading platform, as part of its efforts to invest in the metaverse. The swoosh platform will be used by the world's largest sports apparel company to release its own digital products, which users can collect and showcase. These digital items will come with various tie-ins, and some may grant access to physical items, make them available in video games, or provide entry to real-life events. Registration for the platform opens on November 18.
